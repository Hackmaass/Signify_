PROJECT WRITEUP: Signify

PROJECT NAME: Signify - Your Personal AI-Powered ASL Tutor

OVERVIEW:
Signify is an innovative, gamified education platform designed to make learning American Sign Language (ASL) accessible, interactive, and effective. By leveraging advanced webcam-based hand tracking and state-of-the-art Generative AI, Signify provides a "Live Tutor" experience that watches you sign and offers instant, personalized corrections‚Äîjust like a real teacher.

GOOGLE TECHNOLOGIES IMPLEMENTED:
Signify is built on a robust foundation of Google's cutting-edge tools and services:

1.  üß† **Google Gemini AI Models**:

    *   **Gemini 2.5 Flash Preview (TTS)**: Powers the conversational interface. We utilize the Native Multimodal Live API capabilities (specifically Text-to-Speech) to give the AI a natural, encouraging voice (Voice: 'Kore') that speaks corrections and affirmations to the learner.

KEY FEATURES:

1.  üì∑ **Real-Time Hand Tracking**
    -   Utilizes **Google MediaPipe** for instant client-side skeletal tracking.
    -   Visualizes hand landmarks on screen for immediate visual feedback.
    -   Privacy-focused: Raw video feeds are processed locally; only snapshots are analyzed by Gemini when feedback is requested.

2.  ü§ñ **AI-Powered Live Tutor**
    -   **Vision Analysis**: **Gemini 3 Flash** evaluates the user's hand shape, orientation, and motion against the correct ASL sign.
    -   **Natural Voice**: **Gemini 2.5 Flash TTS** provides spoken feedback, creating a conversational coaching loop.
    -   **Granular Feedback**: Detects subtle errors (e.g., thumb tuck, palm orientation) that traditional classifiers miss.

3.  üìö **Smart Curriculum & Generative Lessons**

    -   **AI Generator**: Type *any* word or phrase, and **Gemini 3** instantly creates a structured lesson plan with descriptions, difficulty ratings, and instructions.

4.  üî• **Gamification & Progress**
    -   **Daily Streaks**: Logic stored in **Firestore** tracks consecutive practice days.
    -   **Quota Tracker**: Visualizes daily AI API usage limits.
    -   **Smart History**: Remembers your past lessons and scores.
